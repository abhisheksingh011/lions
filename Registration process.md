 Registration Process

All BCMCL player registrations for the 2026 season must be completed through the
official BCMCL registration system available on the BCMCL website (www.bcmcl.ca).

1.  A player is considered eligible to play in BCMCL only after:
●  BCMCL online player registration form is initiated.
●  BCMCL player registration form is approved by Club Admin
●  The player registration form is approved by BCMCL League Admin

2.   Registration Categories: Players fall into one of two categories:

1.  Existing (Returning) Players: Players who were registered with any

BCMCL club in the previous BCMCL season, and;

2.  New Players: Players who have not yet created a BCMCL ID

Each category follows a slightly different process.

3. Existing (Returning) Player Registration Process:

 Who Is an Existing BCMCL Player? A player is considered an existing BCMCL player
if they:

●  Were registered with any BCMCL club in the previous BCMCL season, and
●

Intend to play in the 2026 BCMCL season

Step 1: Access BCMCL Registration

●  Visit www.bcmcl.ca
●  Log in using your existing BCMCL player account
●  Confirm full name, date of birth, and contact details including player image
●  Update any changes since last season
●  Select your BCMCL club for the 2026 season
●

If changing clubs, follow the BCMCL Player Transfer Process (Section 5)

Step 2: Club Approval; Club reviews and approves the player

Step 3: BCMCL Approval; BCMCL confirms eligibility and activates the player

✅ Player becomes eligible only after BCMCL approval

4. New Player Registration Process; Who Is a New BCMCL Player?  A player is
considered new to BCMCL if they:

●  Have never played in BCMCL, or
●  Are joining BCMCL for the first time from another league, province, or country

Step-by-Step Process (New BCMCL Players)

Step 1: Create a BCMCL Player Account

●  Visit www.bcmcl.ca
●  Select New Player Registration
●  Enter full legal name and date of birth
●  Contact information
●  Emergency contact details
●  Choose the BCMCL club you will represent for the 2026 season
●  Registration requires a club affiliation

Step 2: BCMCL Verification (Required)

●  BCMCL may request:

○  Proof of age
○  Player clearance if applicable (Juniors)

Step 3: Club & BCMCL Approval

●  Club admin approves the player registration
●  BCMCL league admin provides final approval

✅ Only BCMCL approved players may participate in league matches.

5. BCMCL Player Transfer Process (Changing Clubs)

Existing BCMCL players wishing to change clubs for the 2026 season must:

●  Select the new BCMCL club during registration
●  Submit a BCMCL transfer request
●  Receive approval from:

○  Current BCMCL club by email
○  Accepted by New BCMCL club, and send email to statistician with release
○  BCMCL League Administration approve player transfer

⚠ Players may not play for a new club until the BCMCL transfer is fully approved

6. Registration Deadlines

●  Early Registration Period: (Recommended)
●  Transfer Deadline: June 1, 6:00 PM ( Local Time)

7. BCMCL Eligibility Rules

●  A player must be registered and approved by BCMCL before playing any match
●  Registration with any external body does not grant BCMCL eligibility
●  Clubs fielding unregistered or ineligible players may face:

○  Match forfeiture
○  Fines
○  Disciplinary action

8. Club and League Responsibilities

Player Responsibilities: It is the player responsibility to ensure the provided information
is  accurate.  The  registration  must  be  completed  on  time  by  following  registration  and
transfer  rules  where  applicable.  Only  one  player  ID  is  allowed  in  BCMCL,  and  it  is
Player  responsibility  to  ensure  players  have  only  one  ID.  In  case  of  any  registration
issue the player must reach out to their club president or secretary for assistance.

Club  Responsibilities:  The  club  admin  is  responsible  to  review  and  approve  players
promptly, ensure no unregistered players play, or player does not create duplicate ID. In
case  of  any  issues  the  club  secretary or president can reach out to the league admin.
League  Responsibilities:  The  league  will  oversee  compliance,  maintain  integrity of the
registration process and step in for assistance if required.

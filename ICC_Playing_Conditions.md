## 📰 Key Recent Rule Changes (Oct 2025 onwards)

> Source: [ICC News – Several Changes Made to ICC Playing Conditions](https://www.icc-cricket.com/news/several-changes-made-to-icc-playing-conditions)

- **Stop Clock in Tests:** Bowling team must be ready within 60 seconds of the previous over; failure results in a 5-run penalty (3rd offense per innings).
- **Injury Replacements:** Players with serious on-field injuries can be replaced by a like-for-like player for the remainder of the match.
- **Wide Ball Rule (Trial):** The batter's leg position at the point of delivery is now the reference point for wide calls, not where the batter moves to subsequently.
- **ODI Ball Change:** Reverted to one ball per innings for the last 16 overs.
- **Boundary Catches:** New ruling to clarify boundary catch decisions.
- **DRS Wicket Zone:** Updates to the Decision Review System wicket-hit zone.
- **Concussion Replacements:** Updated process for concussion substitutions.
- **Deliberate Short Runs:** Revised rules around deliberate short runs.
